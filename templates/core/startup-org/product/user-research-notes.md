# User Research Notes

<!-- LLM: Encourage continuous customer conversations. The best products come from deep customer understanding. Help founders synthesize patterns from multiple conversations. Ask: "What surprised you?" "What did you learn?" "How does this change your roadmap?" -->

**Status:** Living | **Update Frequency:** After each user interview/research session
**Primary Roles:** Product Co-founder, Founder/CEO
**Related Documents:** `/product/product-overview.md`, `/strategic/roadmap.md`

## Purpose

Track customer conversations, interviews, and research to understand user needs, validate hypotheses, and guide product decisions. At your stage, customer conversations are your most valuable product input.

**Start-Up Reality:**
- Talk to users every week (ideally every day)
- Track patterns across conversations
- Use insights to drive roadmap, not gut feelings
